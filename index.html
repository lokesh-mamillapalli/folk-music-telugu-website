BTP Telugu Folk Project

Documenting, preserving, and analyzing the rich folk music heritage of Andhra Pradesh & Telangana.

✦

🎵 Project Background

The two South Indian states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana have a rich folk culture that has been less explored academically. This project investigates the diverse musical forms within folk music that are an integral part of society. It is interesting to study the language used in these songs, as it differs from the language of other musical traditions.

The study of melodic and rhythmic structures provides insights into the distinctive musical culture, including commonly used rhythmic patterns and the influence of certain ragas native to these songs.

📚 Collection & Documentation

The project is designed to collect as many songs as possible from multiple sources, such as printed materials, audio recordings, and interactions with artists. A website is being developed featuring the lyrics of songs along with links to both existing and newly recorded performances.

🔍 Core Focus

The website also includes information about musical instruments native to this culture. The core focus of the project is the analysis of linguistic, musical, and cultural aspects of songs, along with systematic documentation of all collected material.
